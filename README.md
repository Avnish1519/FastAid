# FastAid
Helps Saves someones life

In emergencies like heart attacks, choking, or sudden collapse, people often panic and forget what to do. Fast Aid provides instant, step-by-step guidance to help you act quickly and correctly when it matters most.

Designed for situations where time is critical and thinking becomes difficult, Fast Aid delivers clear, simple instructions so you can help yourself or others without confusion.

Stay calm. Act fast. Save lives.
